A vast blue canvas, Sea and sky, a mirror's gleam, Moments, swiftly pass.

Life's a rushing stream, We stand still, in silent thought, Seeds grow, futures
bloom.

Birth and death entwined, Happiness, a passing shade, Truth, a fleeting breath.

Sea and sky reflect, Constant change, yet ever the same, The now, the only
truth.
