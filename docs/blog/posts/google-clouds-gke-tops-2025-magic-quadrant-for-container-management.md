Thrilled to share that for the third year in a row, Gartner has recognized
Google as a Leader in the 2025 Magic Quadrant for Container Management! 🎉

As someone who loves working with Google Cloud, GKE has always been a personal
favorite of mine, right up there with GCLB and Gemini. It's fantastic to see
this powerful tool get the recognition it deserves.

For me, GKE is a game-changer for container orchestration, making it so much
easier to manage and scale containerized applications. It's a key part of
modernizing our infrastructure and embracing a cloud-native approach.
